About levi.art

A journey of artistic excellence, mentorship, and purposeful storytelling through timeless pencil artistry.

The Journey

Founded in 2021 and rebranded in 2023, levi.art has evolved into a premier destination for custom charcoal and graphite portraiture. What started as a passion for capturing human emotion through pencil has grown into a comprehensive artistic experience.

Specializing in personal, couple, family, memorial, and wedding gift portraits, we believe every artwork tells a unique story that celebrates identity, love, memory, and legacy.
