The Artistic Journey

Founded in 2021 and rebranded in 2023, levi.art has evolved from a passionate endeavor into a premier destination for custom charcoal and graphite portraiture. What began as a deep fascination with capturing human emotion through pencil strokes has blossomed into a comprehensive artistic experience that touches lives and preserves memories.

Levi M. Mulama's artistic journey started in the quiet corners of Mombasa, where the interplay of light and shadow first captured his imagination around 2015. Through a decade of dedicated practice and exploration, he developed a unique style that blends technical precision with emotional depth.

2021

Started creating custom portraits for friends and family, discovering the power of personalized art.

2022

Medium Exploration

Explored different mediums and techniques to discover new forms of expression, enhancing creative vision, and developed the adopted black and white style.

2023

Rebranding & Growth

Officially launched levi.art with a renewed vision and expanded service offerings.

2024
