Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about commissioning artwork

How long does a custom portrait take?

Typically 1-2 weeks depending on complexity, size, and current commission queue. Rush options may be available.

What photos do I need to provide?

High-quality, well-lit photos with clear facial features. Multiple angles are helpful. We'll guide you through the selection process.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide with secure packaging and tracking. Shipping costs are calculated based on location.

Can I request changes during the process?

Absolutely! We share progress photos and welcome feedback at key stages to ensure complete satisfaction.
